About

Anatid Systems is a practitioner-led DFIR consultancy. We are hands-on and ready to partner with our customers; we work on issues alongside them. No SOW before we talk. No surprise invoices. Just hard work.

We proudly boast over a decade of experience spanning cloud security, threat intelligence, and incident response at organizations of every size.

Incident response is a skilled trade, not a sales funnel. We do this work because we're good at it, and we stay in it because the work matters.

The company name comes from Anatidae, the biological family containing ducks, geese, and swans. Our mascot is the goose: territorial, collaborative, adaptable, built for endurance, and fiercely protective of what matters.
